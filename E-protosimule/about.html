Who Are We?

We are a family owned clothing brand located in Conakry Guinea
We offer variety of clothes that originates from the fulani culture.
Fulanies make up over 40% of the population in Guinea. Making them known as the largest tribe to reside in Guinea.
All Fulanies come identify with villages called Futa located in rural parts of Guinea.
They are known for their clothing material called Lepi and Bazen.
Our mission statement is to provide you the best quality of Bazen and Lepi that has been sourced by Fulanies in Guinea.
